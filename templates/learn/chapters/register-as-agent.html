Ready to become an official mobile money agent? Here's your complete guide to registration in Cameroon.

üìã Requirements to Register

Personal Documents

  • National ID Card (CNI) - Valid and not expired
  • Passport photos - Recent, typically 4x4 format
  • Proof of residence - Utility bill or locator's certificate

Business Requirements

  • Fixed location - Kiosk, shop, or stable business point
  • Initial capital - Varies by network (50,000 - 200,000 CFA)
  • Mobile phone - Smartphone recommended
  • Valid SIM cards - Registered in your name

üü° Registering with MTN Mobile Money

  1. Visit an MTN Service Center - Find your nearest location
  2. Request Agent Application - Ask for agent registration forms
  3. Submit documents - CNI, photos, proof of residence
  4. Initial deposit - Make your first float purchase
  5. Training - Complete MTN agent training
  6. Get your code - Receive your agent credentials
  7. Start operations - Begin serving customers

üìû MTN Agent Hotline

Call 8181 or visit mtn.cm for current requirements

üü† Registering with Orange Money

  1. Visit Orange Shop or Partner - Authorized registration points
  2. Fill application form - Provide all required information
  3. Submit documentation - CNI and business proof
  4. Verification - Orange verifies your application
  5. Training session - Learn Orange Money operations
  6. Activation - Receive agent SIM and credentials
  7. Start serving - Open for business

üìû Orange Agent Support

Call 950 or visit orange.cm for details
