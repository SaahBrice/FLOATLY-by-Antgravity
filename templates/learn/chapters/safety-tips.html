As a mobile money agent, you handle cash and digital money every day. Protecting yourself and your customers is essential for a sustainable business.

üîê Secure Your Work Environment

Physical Security

  • Position your workspace wisely - Keep your phone screen away from customer view during transactions
  • Secure your cash - Use a lockbox and count money discreetly
  • Be aware of your surroundings - Know your regular customers and watch for suspicious behavior
  • Limit cash on hand - Make regular bank deposits to reduce risk

Digital Security

  • Never share your PIN - Not even with "network staff" calling you
  • Use strong passwords - Different passwords for each SIM/account
  • Enable screen lock - Auto-lock your phone after 30 seconds
  • Update your apps - Keep mobile money apps current for security patches

‚úÖ Transaction Best Practices

Before Every Transaction

  1. Verify the customer's identity - Check ID for large amounts
  2. Confirm the phone number - Read it back to the customer
  3. Check the amount twice - Before pressing send
  4. Verify your float balance - Ensure you have enough

After Every Transaction

  1. Wait for SMS confirmation - Both you and customer should receive it
  2. Record immediately - Log in Floatly before the next customer
  3. Give/take receipt - Keep records of every transaction

üö® Emergency Preparedness

What to Do If Robbed

  • Stay calm - Your safety is more important than money
  • Don't resist - Comply with demands
  • Report immediately - Call police and your network provider
  • Block your lines - Contact MTN/Orange to freeze accounts

Important Numbers to Save

  • Police Emergency - 117 (Cameroon)
  • MTN Customer Care - 8181
  • Orange Customer Care - 950
