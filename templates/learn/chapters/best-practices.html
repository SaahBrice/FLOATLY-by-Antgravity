Running a successful mobile money kiosk requires consistency, organization, and smart habits. Here's how the best agents operate.

üìÖ Daily Routine

Morning Checklist

  1. Check all SIM balances - Know your starting float for each network
  2. Count your cash drawer - Verify cash on hand
  3. Charge your phones - Ensure devices are ready for the day
  4. Review yesterday's records - Catch any errors early
  5. Plan your float - Do you need to refill today?

During Operating Hours

  • Record every transaction immediately - Don't rely on memory
  • Keep transactions organized - Separate different networks
  • Monitor your balances - Don't let float run too low
  • Take breaks strategically - Avoid fatigue errors

End of Day

  1. Reconcile all transactions - Match records to actual balances
  2. Count cash and compare - Should match expected amount
  3. Calculate daily profit - Know how you performed
  4. Secure valuables - Lock up everything properly
  5. Review and plan tomorrow - What float do you need?

Balancing Cash and Float

The "seesaw" principle: When cash goes up, float goes down (and vice versa). Your total assets should stay constant minus profits.
