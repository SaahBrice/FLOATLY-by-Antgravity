üìä Daily Reconciliation

The 5-Minute End-of-Day Check

  1. Count physical cash
  2. Check all SIM balances
  3. Calculate expected total - Starting cash + deposits - withdrawals + profit
  4. Compare to actual - They should match
  5. Investigate any difference - Don't ignore discrepancies
